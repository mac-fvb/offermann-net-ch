Academic work

Dissertation

I submitted my dissertation on British Indian prisons in 2018 to the University of Berne. It has been published in Open Access in 2022, funded by the Swiss National Fund as «Zivilisieren durch Strafen. Britisch-Indiens Gefängnisse in der globalen Wissenszirkulation über die strafende Haft, 1820–1889» (Steiner).

HGIS British India

For my dissertation, I created a historical map of British India and some provinces (Lower Provinces, NWP, Madras, Punjab), ca. 1850 using QGIS. The Shapefile is freely available.
